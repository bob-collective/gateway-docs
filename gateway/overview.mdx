## Why Gateway?

Unlock native Bitcoin liquidity for the entire crypto ecosystem.

Bitcoin holders face significant friction when accessing DeFi opportunities - requiring multiple transactions, bridging steps, and complex wallet interactions. Gateway eliminates these barriers by enabling direct settlement from native BTC into any DeFi position across chains.

Whether you're building a DeFi protocol, wallet, or aggregator - Gateway provides the infrastructure to tap into Bitcoin's $1T+ in liquidity with minimal integration effort.

## What is BOB Gateway?

BOB Gateway is a permissionless intent - and RFQ - based protocol that enables frictionless movement of Bitcoin into DeFi.
It connects Bitcoin holders and DeFi opportunities across chains, enabling settlement of native BTC into any assets or DeFi positions - from Bitcoin to DeFi in a single transaction.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="User Initiates">
    User requests a quote through your app for their desired DeFi action (stake, swap, bridge, etc.)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quote Generation">
    Gateway API provides a quote with routing, fees, and estimated output
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Transaction Signing">
    User signs a single Bitcoin or EVM transaction
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verification & Execution">
    Bitcoin Light Client verifies the transaction on-chain, and the protocol executes the DeFi action
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Token Delivery">
    User receives their tokens or staking position directly in their wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>
